Who is Daniel? Little about me

From here to there

Kuopio is a nice city in Norther Savo. There I was born and lived my childhood. I will always remember Kuopio from its nature, which I think is the most beautiful in Finland. Lots of hills and cliffs where which we used to climb and green nature by the Kallavesi -lake. I recommend everyone to visit Puijo if you go to Kuopio. Its nature is peaceful and pure.

I was 14 years old when me and my parents moved to live in Oulu. It isn't never nice to move when you are in that age but I managed and got lots of good friends. In Oulu studied in the Merikoski highschool and graduated in 2010. After that I worked in various places, Cashier at Bauhaus co. Customer servant at Stockmann Oy and as a gymnasium teacher for elementary school boys. I also compeleted my military service at Sodankylä and will always remember the night that there was 38 degrees celsius and we were in the wilderness camping!

Now I live in Joensuus with my beautiful fionce. It's almost on the same latitude as Kuopio so I really enjoy it after the flat swamps of Oulu. Joensuu has threated us well and raised a little bit traveling feaver in me. So I hope that next time I move it would be abroad but not too far. Swedan or Norway would sound just about right
